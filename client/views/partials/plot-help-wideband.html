Controls

Left Click
Tune to selected frequency
Left-Click Drag
Zoom into the plot
Right Click
Undo the most recent zoom
Drag the Accordion
Tune to the selected frequency
Hover Accordion near Plot Edge
Tune through the wide band spectrum continuously (faster when closer to the edge)
Click Arrows On Plot
Tune to the next lower or higher wide band spectrum segment
Middle Click (or Press M Key)
Show the plotting menu
Press F Key
Show the plot in fullscreen
