Controls

Left Click
Tune to selected frequency
Left-Click Drag
Zoom into the plot
Right Click
Undo the most recent zoom
Drag the Selection Box
Tune to selected frequency
Drag Selection Box To Plot Edge
Tune slowly through the wide band spectrum continuously
Drag Selection Box Past Plot Edge
Tune rapidly through the wide band spectrum continuously
Click Arrows On Plot
Tune to the next lower or higher wide band spectrum segment
Middle Click (or Press M Key)
Show the plotting menu
Press F Key
Show the plot in fullscreen
