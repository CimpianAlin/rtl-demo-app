Controls

Left Click
Tune to the frequency under the mouse
Left-Click Drag
Zoom into the plot
Right Click
Undo the most recent zoom
Drag the Accordion
Tune to the frequency under the center of the accordion
Hover the Accordion over Either Wide Band Plot Edge
Tune through the wide band spectrum continuously (faster when closer to the edge)
Click the Arrow Buttons On Either Side of the Wide Band Plot
Tune to the next lower or higher wide band spectrum segment
Middle Click
Show the plotting menu
F
Show the plot in fullscreen
